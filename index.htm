Welcome to my portfolio.

I'm a self-taught illustrator, overcame financial and more challenges created by communist government system. I moved to the US at age 15. Inspired many old and modern cartoons I watched since I was little and find full unrestricted resources by myself as I grew older thanks to the internet.

I have worked in a variety of techniques such as traditional and digital art, storyboarding, and storytelling. I can also animate quite well, and most of the time, I excel in art direction myself.

If you have any questions, have a particular project that you would like to discuss, or if you just want to reach out and say hello, do not hesitate to email me.
